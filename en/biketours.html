Over Mij

My name is Luc, I am 18 years old. I have been a member of the NJN since 2016. In the years that followed, I became very interested in nature. At the moment I find Diptera (flies) and Hymenoptera (bees, wasps and ants) the most interesting. I enjoy trying out and learning many new things, such as creating this website. Many of those little try-outs can be found on this site.
